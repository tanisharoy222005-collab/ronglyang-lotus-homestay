Welcome To Ronglyang Lotus Homestay

Located in Upper Syari, Gangtok, Ronglyang Lotus Homestay provides a peaceful retreat surrounded by mountains and greenery. Guests enjoy spacious accommodation, balcony rooms, free parking, high-speed WiFi and easy access to Gangtok's most popular tourist attractions including MG Marg, Tsomgo Lake, Banjhakri Falls and Ganesh Tok.
