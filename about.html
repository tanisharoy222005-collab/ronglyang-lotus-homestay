About Ronglyang Lotus Homestay

Experience authentic Sikkimese hospitality, beautiful mountain views and peaceful surroundings.

Our Story

Nestled amidst the scenic hills of Gangtok, Ronglyang Lotus Homestay was created to offer guests a peaceful retreat away from the busy city life while remaining close to popular attractions.

Our homestay combines comfort, nature and hospitality, allowing travelers to experience the beauty of Sikkim through warm service, comfortable rooms and breathtaking views.

Whether you're visiting Gangtok for leisure, family vacations, romantic getaways or business trips, we strive to make every stay memorable.
