About Ronglyang Lotus Homestay

Experience authentic Sikkimese hospitality, beautiful mountain views and peaceful surroundings.

Our Story

Nestled in the beautiful hills of Gangtok, Ronglyang Lotus Homestay offers travelers a peaceful retreat with breathtaking mountain views and warm hospitality.

Our property is designed to provide guests with comfort, nature and a memorable Sikkim experience while staying close to the city's major attractions.

Whether you're visiting for leisure, family vacations, honeymoons or business trips, our team strives to make every stay relaxing and enjoyable.
